Diwali lights problem
Given a list of glow timing of lights in seconds , find the time in seconds when they all will glow at the same time.
If  there is no possible way  that all lights can glow together then simply print -1.

Example:
Input: 2
	1 2 3 4 
	10 20 45 26
Output: 12
	   2340
Explanation:
	Test case 1: The first light will glow after every 1 second, Second light will glow after every 2 seconds, Third light will glow after every 3 seconds, Fourth light will glow after every 4 seconds then on the 12th second all the lights will glow together.
